03 — Pro Tips
💡
Resume Writing Tips for Research Projects
TIP 01
Use Action Verbs
Start every bullet with: Designed, Developed, Architected, Engineered, Built, Implemented, Researched — not "Worked on" or "Helped with".
TIP 02
Add Numbers
Even for research: "10 patent diagrams", "sub-200ms latency", "85% detection accuracy". Numbers make bullets 3× more credible.
TIP 03
Patent = Major Highlight
Add a separate "Publications & IP" section. Write: "Patent Application filed — AR-Bridge Remote Repair System (2025)". This is rare and impressive for UG students.
TIP 04
Link Your GitHub
Put your GitHub URL prominently in header. Recruiters click it. A well-maintained repo with a README like this one signals serious engineering thinking.
TIP 05
"Research Stage" is Fine
Write "Research & Architecture Phase" — never say "incomplete". Framing matters. Patent-stage research is more valuable than a half-built CRUD app.
TIP 06
Tailor to Job Role
For AR/XR jobs: lead with Unity & ARCore bullets. For ML roles: lead with RCNN & TensorFlow bullets. For backend roles: lead with Firebase & WebRTC.
